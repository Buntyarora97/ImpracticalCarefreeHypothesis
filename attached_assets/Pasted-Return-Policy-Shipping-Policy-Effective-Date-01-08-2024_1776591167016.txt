Shipping Policy
Effective Date: 01/08/2024



1. Shipping Information
1.1 Processing Time
All orders placed on glimlach.in are processed within 3-5 business days. You will receive an email notification once your order has been dispatched.



1.2 Shipping Methods
We offer reliable shipping options for all product categories including Cleaning Agents, Hand Sanitizers, Personal Care & Hygiene, Skin Care, and Wellness & Supplements.

 Shipping times and charges may vary based on your location and the shipping method selected at checkout.

2. Shipping Costs
2.1 Domestic Shipping
For orders below ₹300, a shipping charge of ₹80 will be applied.
For orders above ₹300, standard shipping is free.
Cash on Delivery (COD) is available, and a COD fee of ₹50 will be applicable on all orders above ₹300.
2.2 International Shipping
International shipping is currently not available on glimlach.in.

3. Delivery Times
3.1 Domestic Delivery
Delivery timelines vary based on the chosen shipping method and your location. Estimated delivery times will be displayed at checkout.



3.2 International Delivery
International delivery is currently not available on glimlach.in.

4. Order Tracking
Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. This can be used to track your package through the shipping carrier’s online portal.



5. Shipping Issues
5.1 Lost or Stolen Packages
Glimlach is not responsible for lost or stolen packages after they have been marked as delivered by the courier.

 For assistance, please contact the carrier directly.



5.2 Incorrect Address
Please double-check your shipping address before placing an order.

 We are not responsible for delays or extra charges caused by inaccurate or incomplete addresses.
